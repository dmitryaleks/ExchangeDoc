Off-hours Closing Price Trade

This is a system to provide additional trading opportunities to investors, after-hours and for some time prior to opening time, at the closing price of the day subject to application of time priority principle.


Following are details of off-hours closing price trade.

Off-hours Closing Price Trade
Classification Details
Subject Stock Certificate, ETF, ETN, KDR(Excluding unexecuted issues of the day)
Trading Hours 08:30~08:40, 15:40~16:00 (30 minutes)
Quotation Receiving Hours 08:30~08:40, 15:30~16:00 (40 minutes)
Executed Price Closing price of the day(after market closing), closing price of previous day(before market opening)
Type Of Order Order at closing price
Execution Of Trading Application of time priority rule
Revision, Withdrawal Impossible to revise order price
possible to revise quantity or cancel prior to execution of trading
Trading Unit 1 share
Off-hours Single Price Trade

Off-hours single price trade is a system to provide additional trading opportunities to investors, for a certain time after-hours, through single price auction by every 10 minute at the price within ± 10% of closing price of the day (subject to within upper & lower limit price of the day). The trading is conducted in every 10 minute, and Random End shall be applied.


Following are details of off-hours single price trade.

Off-hours Single Price Trade
Classification Details
Subject Stock Certificate, ETF, ETN, KDR(Excluding unexecuted issues of the day)
Trading Hours 16:00~18:00 (120 minutes)
Method Of Trading Periodic call auction trading regularly per 10 minutes
Range Of Fluctuation ±10% of closing price of the day (subject to within upper limit/lower limit price of the day)
Trading Unit 1 share
Type Of Order Limit order
