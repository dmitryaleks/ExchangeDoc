Trading of Liquidation Issues
  • The Exchange allows the securities scheduled to be delisted to be traded within 7 days to provide the shareholders with the last opportunity to sell the securities.
  • Traded via limit order only through single price auctions with the interval of 30 minutes (14 times in total) without daily price limits during regular trading session.
  • During off-hours trading, the trading by last traded price is applied to the same daily price limits as normal issue trading but the trading by a single price auction has no daily price limits.
